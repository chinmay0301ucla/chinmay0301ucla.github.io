<div class="col-lg-5">
<h2 class="text-center">Mid-Air<br></h2>
</div>
</div>
<div class="row justify-content-center" style="margin-top:28px;">
<div class="col-lg-7 text-justify">
<p>
<b>Mid-Air</b>, The <i>Montefiore Institute Dataset of Aerial Images and Records</i>, is a multi-purpose synthetic
dataset for low altitude drone flights. It provides a large amount of synchronized data corresponding to flight records
for multi-modal vision sensors and navigation sensors mounted on board of a flying quadcopter. Our multi-modal vision sensors capture
RGB pictures, relative surface normal orientation, depth, object semantics and stereo disparity.</p>
<p>
Additionally, each flight trajectory was recorded several times in the same place but in different climate conditions in order to change the visuals of the scene.
This offers the opportunity to train algorithms for robustness to visual changes. A test set for benchmarking this particular criteria is
proposed alongside the training data.</p>
</div>
<div class="col-lg-5">
<div class="embed-responsive embed-responsive-16by9 " style="margin-top:5px;">

</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="row justify-content-center" style="margin-top:28px;margin-bottom: 28px;">
<div class="col-lg-5">
<h2 class="text-center">Features</h2>
</div>
</div>
<div class="row equal justify-content-lg-start" >
<div class="col-lg-auto my-auto" style="background-image: linear-gradient(rgba(52,58,64,0.35), rgba(52,58,64,0.35)), url('img/screen-1.jpg');background-size: cover;background-position: center center;">
<br>
<span class="text-light text-center"><h2 class="display-4">420k</h2><h1 class="display-6">frames</h1></span>
<hr style="border-top: 2px solid white;">
<span class="text-light text-center"><h2 class="display-4">79min</h2><h1 class="display-6">flight</h1></span>
<br>
</div>
<div class="col-lg-6 text-justify">
<hr>
<h2>Large training set</h2>
<p>
Our dataset contains 79 minutes of drone flight records extracted out of more than 5 hours of flight records.
Records were captured by manually flying the drone in a virtual environment thanks to an RC controller connected to the computer.
The 79 minutes of flight are divided into 54 individual trajectories of equal length.
</p>
<p>
Since each trajectory is rendered several times for different climate scenarios, Mid-Air offers more
than 420,000 individual training frames.
</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="row equal justify-content-lg-end flex-column-reverse flex-lg-row h-100">
<div class="col-lg-6">
<div class="text-justify">
<hr>
<h2>Multi-modal sensors</h2>
<p>
One of the important features of Mid-Air is the types of data which are proposed. Our drone is equipped with 3 RGB cameras
and records several ground-truth visual maps such as relative surface normal orientation, depth, object semantics, and stereo disparity.
</p>
<p>
On top of that, our dataset provides drone positioning information. Additionally to ground truths, flight
records also contain data logs for several simulated positioning sensors, <i>i.e.</i> accelerometer, gyroscope and GPS.
</p>
<p>
Complete information related to sensor features can be found on the <a href="tech_specs.html">Technical specifications page</a>.
</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="col-lg-auto " style="background-image: linear-gradient(rgba(52,58,64,0.35), rgba(52,58,64,0.35)), url('img/screen-2.jpg');background-size: cover;background-position: center center;">
<br>
<span class="text-light text-center"><h2 class="display-4">3</h2><h1 class="display-6">cameras</h1></span>
<hr style="border-top: 2px solid white;">
<span class="text-light text-center"><h1 class="display-6">IMU</h1><h1 class="display-6">GPS</h1></span>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<div class="row equal justify-content-lg-start">
<div class="col-lg-auto my-auto " style="background-image: linear-gradient(rgba(52,58,64,0.35), rgba(52,58,64,0.35)), url('img/screen-3.jpg');background-size: cover;background-position: center center;">
<br>
<span class="text-light text-center"><h2 class="display-4">7</h2><h1 class="display-6">climates</h1></span>
<hr style="border-top: 2px solid white;">
<span class="text-light text-center"><h2 class="display-4">3</h2><h1 class="display-6">maps</h1></span>
<br>
</div>
<div class="col-lg-6 text-justify">
<hr>
<h2>Train for robustness</h2>
<p>
With its 4 weather setups, 3 different seasons, 3 environment maps, and high quality visuals, our dataset should give a good insight
on potential performances which can be expected from algorithms in real-world scenarios.
</p>
<p>
Additionally, since each trajectory is recorded several times in different climate conditions, Mid-Air can be used
to test the robustness of vision algorithms to visual changes. By the way, we propose a benchmark designed to
assess the latter.
</p>
<p>
All considerations taken into account during the design phase of the dataset and the benchmark are explained in
detail in <a href="https://orbi.uliege.be/bitstream/2268/234665/3/Fonder2019MidAir.pdf">
our paper</a>.
</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="row equal justify-content-lg-end flex-column-reverse flex-lg-row">
<div class="col-lg-6 text-justify">
<hr>
<h2>Ease of use</h2>
<p>
We wanted to reduce the time required to set up our dataset as much as possible. We did this by choosing a straightforward
<a href="data_organization.html">Data organization</a>. We also provide simple example
scripts ( <a href="https://github.com/montefiore-ai/midair-dataset/" onclick="ga('send', 'event', 'Link', 'Click', 'Goto Github');"><i class="fa fa-external-link" aria-hidden="false"></i>GitHub</a>) to show how to use it.
</p>
<p>
Additionally, we have an automated download procedure that lets you finely select what you want to download.
Our <a href="download.html">Download page</a> also contains a small preview
for each trajectory so that you can get an idea of its content without having to download it completely.
</p>
</div>
