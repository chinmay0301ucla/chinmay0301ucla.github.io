Discovery Challenge Leaderboard

We are hosting the discovery challenge to motivate the development of state of the art methods to learn underlying physics phenomena from videos in an interpretable manner. The complexity of the tasks is determined by the number of independent variables the method is supposed to learn and the complexity of the ground truth equation. The total score to a submission will be evaluated on the number of tasks for which the submitted method is able to discover the underlying physics, and the overall time taken for the method.
